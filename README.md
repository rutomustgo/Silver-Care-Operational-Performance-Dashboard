# Silver-Care-Operational-Performance-Dashboard
To provide data-driven insights into patient arrival patterns, financial contributions, and stay durations.
📊 Project Overview
This project transforms raw patient arrival and revenue data from Silver Care Hospital into an interactive executive dashboard. The goal is to provide hospital leadership with a 360-degree view of operational efficiency, revenue distribution, and patient load patterns to optimize staffing and resource allocation.
🚀 Key Strategic Insights
The dashboard identifies four critical pillars of the hospital's current operational state:

Temporal Patient Load: Identification of a "Pressure Zone" between 08:00 and 11:00, where nearly 40% of daily volume occurs, peaking at 4,297 arrivals.

Efficiency Bottlenecks: A significant spike in stay duration occurs every Monday (49-minute average), identifying a specific day for process intervention.

Revenue Mix: Insurance (40%) and Corporate (23%) classes represent the primary financial anchors of the facility.

Service Category Value: Clear visibility into Standard vs. High-Value service contributions, ensuring transparency across all revenue streams.
🛠️ Technical Implementation
Data Cleaning: Standardized arrival times, calculated stay durations in minutes, and categorized financial classes using Excel Power Query and formulas.

Pivot Analysis: Utilized multi-dimensional PivotTables to aggregate revenue by category and average stay duration by day of the week.

UI/UX Design: Implemented a "Dark Mode" dashboard container with white "Information Cards," utilizing minimalist design principles (Zero-Chrome Interface) for maximum readability.

Visualizations:

Pie Chart: Revenue contribution by financial class.

Histogram-style Column Chart: Hourly patient distribution.

Smoothed Line Chart: Trend analysis of stay duration.

Horizontal Bar Chart: Direct-labeled revenue category breakdown.
